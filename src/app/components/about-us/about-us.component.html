About us

A compass shows the way to the destination. City Compass provides orientation in the complex global business world - with intercultural consulting and mentoring, executive coaching and training, and with a profound network in Romania and Germany.

  Volker Moser and Christian Pauls, the founding members of City Compass, started themselves as expats in Bucharest and have been serving the expat community since 2007.

They share their many years of experience with people and companies from different cultures with competence and passion - in Romania, Germany, Europe and around the world.
